Stay Safe Online Checklist:

- Never share OTP or PIN with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Use official banking apps only
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links
- Verify caller identity before sharing details
- Enable transaction alerts
- Regularly check bank statements
- Use strong passwords for apps

If you become a victim, report immediately:
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline Number: 1930
